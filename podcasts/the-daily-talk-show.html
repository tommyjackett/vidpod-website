About
the show

The Daily Talk Show was a raw, honest, story-driven daily podcast — family, business, relationships, growth, and everything in between. Tommy co-hosted with his best mate Josh Janssen for over 1,000 episodes.

The show had notable guests drop in including Hamish Blake, Sam Mac, and Christian O\u2019Connell, and was covered regularly by Radio Today.

It\u2019s where Tommy built the muscle that now powers VIDPOD. Ten years of daily recording, editing, publishing and showing up — turns out that\u2019s pretty good training for a production company.
