About
the show

The Numbers Game is one of Australia's fastest-growing finance podcasts — real talk about money, investing, property, and building wealth, hosted by Nick Bracks and Martin Mulcare and joined by a rotating cast of guests from finance, business and media.

VIDPOD is the show's full production partner. We run the recording sessions in-studio, cut the long-form video, build every episode's show notes, create thumbnails and social assets, and handle distribution across YouTube, Spotify, Apple and everywhere in between.

It's a full-pipeline gig: the kind of show where every episode goes through multiple craft stages — and every one of them runs through our team.
