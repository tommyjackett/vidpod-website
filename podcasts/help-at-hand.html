About
the show

Help at Hand is a branded podcast series built to do one thing properly: tell real, useful stories about the people and moments that matter. It's the kind of show you make when you want the content to outlast the campaign.

VIDPOD produced the series end-to-end — pre-production planning, remote recording setups across multiple locations, full edit, cinematic treatment, and the whole publishing pipeline.

This is the kind of branded podcast we love: a client who cares about the craft as much as we do, and a show that's built to actually mean something to its audience.
