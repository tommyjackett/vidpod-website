About
the show

Carlisle Homes is one of Victoria\u2019s most respected home builders. Their podcast gives buyers, designers and industry people an inside look at what actually goes into building a great home.

VIDPOD produces the show end-to-end — planning, recording, editing, publishing, and all the content assets that go around it. A strong example of branded podcasting done with care.

Content like this is where branded podcasts earn their keep — useful, trusted, long-lasting conversations that sit next to a brand\u2019s best work.
