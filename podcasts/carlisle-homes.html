About the show

Home Files is Carlisle Homes' branded podcast for anyone thinking about building. Whether you're knocking down an existing home to rebuild, or stepping into the property market for the first time, the show is designed to take the guesswork out of the journey.

Across two seasons, Home Files sits down with industry experts, Carlisle's own specialists, and real customers to unpack each phase of the build, from assessing your block and setting a budget, through to choosing a design and styling the finished place. Season 2 is hosted by Caitlin Judd, guiding first home buyers through the whole process with a warm, practical tone.

Published by Carlisle Homes.

Production journey

Two seasons, two audiences

A branded podcast that evolved from knockdown rebuild stories to a dedicated first home buyer series.

Season 1 · Knockdown Rebuild
Launching the show
Six episodes built around the knockdown rebuild audience. Designing the home you want, making finance less scary, insta-worthy interiors, sustainable builds that save money, and a real customer's Affinity home journey. VIDPOD set the format, the sound, and the production pipeline.
Season 2 · First Home Buyer
New host, new audience
Host Caitlin Judd took the mic for a dedicated first home buyer series. Teaser plus five episodes walking buyers through choosing to build, affordability and finance, maximising your block, styling on a budget, and a step-by-step guide to the whole building process. Tighter focus, same production bar.
Ongoing
Live on the Carlisle hub
Both seasons live on Carlisle's own podcast page at carlislehomes.com.au, plus major audio platforms. Content that keeps doing work long after it's published.
