About
the show

Let's Talk Teaching is the Monash Education Academy's flagship podcast — long-form, carefully crafted conversations with educators, researchers and thought leaders about what actually works in the classroom and the lecture theatre.

VIDPOD partners with Monash to produce the whole thing: studio recording, full edit, show notes, cover art, and publishing. The brief is high-quality and considered — every detail has to reflect the care Monash puts into its teaching practice.

It's one of our favourite shows to work on because the conversations genuinely change how people teach. That's the kind of work we like being part of.
