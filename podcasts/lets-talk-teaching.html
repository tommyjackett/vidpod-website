The show

About the show

Let's Talk Teaching is for educators, about educators, grounded in the real challenges of Australian classrooms.

Each episode, host Rebecca Cooper sits down with Monash researchers and practising teachers to unpack a single topic in depth. From trauma-informed practice to AI in education, game-based learning to the manosphere, feedback to play in the early years.

Warm, practical, peer-to-peer. Published by Monash University Faculty of Education.
