Education · Monash University

The podcast created for teachers, by teachers. Hosted by Associate Professor Rebecca Cooper from the Monash University Faculty of Education.

Let's Talk Teaching is Monash's flagship education podcast — a space for honest, practical conversations about what teaching actually looks like today. Each episode brings together researchers, classroom teachers and school leaders to unpack the issues shaping the profession, from trauma-informed practice and student wellbeing to AI in the classroom, game-based learning and the manosphere.

VIDPOD support

We've been the production partner for Let's Talk Teaching since day one. Studio recording with Bec and her guests, full edit in DaVinci Resolve, episode descriptions and show notes, artwork, social cutdowns, and distribution across Spotify, Apple and Amazon.

Alongside Monash's content team, we help shape each season's line-up and lock in the format. Warm, practical, peer-to-peer — built to actually sound like teachers talking to teachers.
