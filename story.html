Career arc

How I
got here

2013 — 2014
Breakfast radio · Shepparton
Two years on air at Star FM. 15 hours of live radio a week. Hosted solo, talked to farmers, parents, tradies, kids. Where my conversational instinct was forged — on the fly, on the mic, at 4:30 in the morning.
2015
The Daily Talk Show begins
Josh Janssen and I started recording a daily podcast about our real lives. 1,000+ episodes later we'd built one of Australia's longest-running independent shows. Hamish Blake, Sam Mac, Christian O'Connell all passed through.
2016 — 2018
The viral years
Counted the 1,000 Steps. Fished the oBikes. Poured the $3 cup inside the $2 cup. Multiple appearances on The Project. Covered by the ABC, News.com.au, Mashable. The "do the thing nobody else bothered to do" formula became a signature.
2018 — now
VIDPOD · building the business
Turned a personal brand and a network into a production company. Clients across education, finance, fitness, and tech. Word-of-mouth only. Running lean, running sharp, and making work I'd actually watch.
2022
The e-scooter sequel
Same format, five years later. Pulled five dumped e-scooters out of the river in under two hours. Went live on 3AW with Neil Mitchell. Proof that a good idea doesn't expire.
Now
Producing The Numbers Game + AI at the edge
Producing The Numbers Game podcast and a slate of brand work. Currently building at the intersection of AI and production — the next "1,000 Steps" is whatever nobody's thought to count yet.
