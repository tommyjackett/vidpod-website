Career arc

How I
got here

2013 — 2014
Breakfast radio · Shepparton
Two years on air at Star FM. 15 hours of live radio a week. Hosted solo, talked to farmers, parents, tradies, kids. Where my conversational instinct was forged — on the fly, on the mic, at 4:30 in the morning.
2015
The Daily Talk Show begins
Josh Janssen and I started recording a daily podcast about our real lives. 1,000+ episodes later we'd built one of Australia's longest-running independent shows. Hamish Blake, Sam Mac, Christian O'Connell all passed through.
2018 — now
VIDPOD · building the business
Turned a personal brand and a network into a video and podcast production company. Clients across education, finance, fitness, and tech. Word-of-mouth only. Running lean, running sharp, and making work I'd actually watch.
Now
Still having a crack
Building VIDPOD into the production partner I wish I'd had when I started. Working on the shows, the films, and the brand work that shape how I think. Currently building at the edge of AI and production — the next "1,000 Steps" is whatever nobody's thought to count yet.
